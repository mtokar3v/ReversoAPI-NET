לִבְרוֹחַ

livroakh

Print

Model: לָקַחַת

Group Qal

Binyan qal (also known as binyan pa’al) is the most common binyan. It does not have any prefix in past or present tenses, and there is no pattern to the meaning of verbs in this binyan
